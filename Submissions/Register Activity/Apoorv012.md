### Logic:
#### Name:
Should contain only capital letters, small letters, and spaces.
#### Email:
Should match the following regex: ^[\w-\.]+@([\w-]+\.)+[\w-]{2,4}$
#### Password:
Should be at least 8 characters long and at most 20 characters.
Can contain a combination of:
- Capital letters (A-Z)
- Small letters (a-z)
- Special characters (@, #, $, %)
- Numbers (0-9)
